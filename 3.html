Your Name

I know it is a quite common name but you have changed my perception of this common name forever. I will never read or hear your name somewhere and not think of my endless love for you. Every time I hear someone say that name I feel my brain stop for a second and ignore everything else. The only thing passing through my mind for a split second is you and my love for you. I will never get enough of saying it. I feel like it suits my voice too but that's up for debate. I have never felt more warmth from saying something so simple like a name.
