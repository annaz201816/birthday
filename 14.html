Your Hugs

I think it took me a while to realize how strong and powerful hugs can be. The way you hug me is nothing like I've ever felt before. And if I'm honest and a little selfish I hope no one else gets the same kind of hugs I do. I could spend my entire life in your arms. I even remember the first time you hugged me, which wasn't the same as now of course but I still felt so happy. I don't know why it felt like the cutest thing ever, I think it has partly to do with your height. I feel like the whole world could be falling apart next to me but I would find comfort in your arms. I only hope I can give the same to you. I really hope that my hugs and my body can give you the same sensation as yours do to me.
