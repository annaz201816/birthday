Your Devotion to the People You Love

I think this is pretty common but I've never seen someone completely change with me when they took me as an important part of their life. I finally saw how loving and devoted you were to your favorite people. I was just glad I was a part of it. I think you have a special way of caring for people when you perceive them differently. You do have a habit of not caring about a lot of things but that completely changes once you're with someone you love. Till this day I can feel you try your hardest with me to make sure I'm happy.
