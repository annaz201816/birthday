How Easy You Make it to Love you

I know I've said this a couple of times in the past and it is still my favorite way to describe you. You are the easiest person I have ever loved in my life. I think love comes really easy but ends up being hard to work for. I know I will never lose my love for you. I know nothing you do will make me even consider not loving you. Sometimes it makes me mad that I can't control that part of me but that's really what love is. I love how I have to put no effort into loving you, it feels like a natural instinct, like something I was born to do. It is so easy to look at you and be filled with joy which is partly because of the twelve other reasons I talked about before this but there is something about your personality that makes someone feel comfortable around you. And that comfort slowly turns into love.
