Your Presence

This is the last reason of this list but I could go on for hours about why I love you. I remember a couple of weeks ago we were not in the best place and you asked me to tell you why I love you. And I hesitated and I know you thought I can't even think of a reason, but the truth is I could. I don't know why I become the person I am during difficult times. But that's not the point right now. I made this list to show you how I can explain every part of you that I love in detail. And this brings me to the last thing- your presence. I think everything I said before this can be combined into this reason. But you are the only person on the planet whose presence I need and seek for every day. I think I took it for granted when I had it before all this distance between us. But the only time I feel pure joy and true happiness is when I know you're near me. The only thing I can even compare that feeling to is the peace I had when I was a little girl. I didn't think I could ever feel that way again but you are a walking radiation of love and comfort for me. You are my lover, my best friend, and my baby.
